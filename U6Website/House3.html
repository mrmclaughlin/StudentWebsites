The balcony is lined with cast-iron railings, and looks out over the entire property, including an oldgrowth forest, a buetiful lake, and a babbling stream.

Master Bedroom

This is the parent's bedroom. It contains a King-sized bed, and it borders the walk-in closet and the balcony. There is a hardwood floor, and many windows to fill the room with natural light.

Walk-in Closet

Positioned to be easily accessible from the Master Bedroom, this closet is the size of the children's bedrooms. It's shelves are designed to organize clothing based on season, color, and gender-association.

Master Bathroom

This room contains a multidirectional shower, and a small Jacuzzi. The floors are made of white tile, and there is a skylight in the ceiling to provide natural light without compromising anyone's privacy.

Trampoline Room

The room's floor, ceiling, and walls are made of trampolines in front of soundproof foam to silence the springs. Although this room is for the children, it is put on the parent's floor so that, in the event of an injury, adults are nearby.

Observatory

This room is centered around a very large pivoting telescope that looks out of the glass ceiling. It is large and dark, to mimic the night sky.
